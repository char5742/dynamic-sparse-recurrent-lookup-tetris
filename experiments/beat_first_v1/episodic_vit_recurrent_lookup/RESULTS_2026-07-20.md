## Allocation and throughput repair

The repaired 34-parameter postphase exposed type erasure through
`Dict{Symbol,Array{Float32}}`.  Scalar SIMD loops dynamically dispatched and
boxed values, including a Bool reduction in the canonical `cell_bias` replay.
Typed array-rank barriers and a single-pass replay removed this cost without
changing model or optimizer semantics.

## Interpretation and limitations

- The fixed-batch gate proves that the repaired architecture can learn and
  memorize the real teacher signal.
- The scheduler benchmark and production segment both exceed the accepted
  15 updates/s floor.
- The 20,000-update run deliberately used training-only/benchmark mode so no
  validation or sealed seed was touched.  Consequently this run has no honest
  final held top-1, NDCG, margin, or PreAct-difference claim yet.
- A held comparison must be authorized and run separately before claiming that
  this model exceeds PreAct at equal state, compute, or wall-clock budget.
